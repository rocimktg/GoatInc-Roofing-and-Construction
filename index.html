What We Do

Roofing & Exterior Services

From full roof replacements to precision repairs — we handle every layer of your home's exterior with craftsmanship and care.

Roof Replacement

Full tear-off and replacement using premium IKO, CertainTeed, and Class 3 impact-resistant shingles. We inspect the decking, upgrade ventilation, and leave nothing to chance.

Roof Repairs

Storm damage, missing shingles, failed pipe boots, or worn flashing — we find the source and fix it right. Emergency repairs available when your home can't wait.

Free Roof Inspections

Not sure about your roof's condition? We come out, get on the roof, and give you an honest assessment with photos. No pressure, no gimmicks — just facts about your home.

Siding

Vinyl, fiber cement, wood, metal, and stone siding installation and repair. We protect your home's exterior while giving it a sharp, finished look built for the Texas climate.

Fascia & Soffits

Rotten or hail-damaged fascia boards and soffits are a common find during our inspections. We replace them properly in aluminum, steel, fiber cement, or wood — matched to your home.

Chimney Repair & Flashing

Improperly sealed chimney flashing is one of the top causes of roof leaks. We install new stepped and counter flashing, custom chimney caps, and real carpentry crickets — done right.

Gutters

We install and repair gutters to protect your foundation and landscaping from runoff. Properly functioning gutters are the last line of defense for your roof system.

Emergency Storm Response

Central Texas storms move fast. We deploy emergency tarps, secure blown-off shingles, and stop leaks before they become interior damage — often the same day you call.

Skylights & Solar Vents

Skylight installation, solar attic fan upgrades, and ventilation improvements that reduce heat buildup and extend the life of your roof system.
